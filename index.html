Section 1: Arithmetic Aptitude

Q1. A train running at the speed of 60 km/hr crosses a pole in 9 seconds. What is the length of the train?

💡 Explanation: Speed = $60 \times \frac{5}{18} = \frac{50}{3}$ m/sec.
Length of train = $\text{Speed} \times \text{Time} = \frac{50}{3} \times 9 = 150$ meters.

Q2. The average of 20 numbers is calculated as 35. It was discovered later that a number 85 was misread as 45. What is the correct average?

💡 Explanation: Difference in value = $85 - 45 = 40$.
Increase in average = $\frac{40}{20} = 2$.
Correct Average = $35 + 2 = 37$.

Q3. A sum of money fetches a total simple interest of ₹1,200 at the rate of 8% per annum in 5 years. What is the principal sum?

💡 Explanation: $\text{SI} = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100} \implies 1200 = \frac{P \times 8 \times 5}{100} \implies 1200 = \frac{40P}{100}$.
Therefore, $P = \frac{1200 \times 100}{40} = \text{₹}3,000$.

Section 2: Technical & Core Computing

Q4. Which of the following execution contexts handles absolute layout positioning overlays dynamically within basic browser viewports?

💡 Explanation: `position: fixed;` removes an element from the normal document layout flow and positions it relative to the browser viewport boundary coordinates, keeping it static during page scrolling.

Q5. In JavaScript, which array method returns a brand new collection containing elements that pass a specific conditional criteria block?

💡 Explanation: `array.filter()` loops through an collection and evaluates a callback expression statement, constructing a shallow copy array populated only with matching values.
